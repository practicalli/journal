## Cycling

Completed a detailed review for the Medio Fondo route of the Dragon Ride.

Created a doc that lists potential hazards, e.g. cattle grids, blind give-way signs and tight turns.

Also noted interesting places to take a picture of breathtaking scenery.

The location of the two feed-stations were found (I believe).  Its hard to know for certain as the ride website doesn't say where the feed stations are, except for a distance along the route.

There was only one sensible location for the two fee-stations I found when looking on Google Maps, so fairly confident that I found the correct locations.
